Featured Artists

Christine Plubeau

Viola da Gamba

Christine Plubeau has appeared at festivals throughout France and the rest of Europe, in the Lebanon and in Brazil.

Claire Antonini

Theorbo and Lute

Claire Antonini gives regular recitals in France and abroad. She contributes greatly to the dissemination of lute music.

Yoko Nakamura

Harpsichord and Organ

Yoko Nakamura is a laureate of the Tokyo University of Arts.

Emilia Gliozzi

Cello

Emilia Gliozzi has worked with leading early music ensembles, including La Fenice, les Talens Lyriques and the Ensemble Artaserse.

Jérémie Papasergio

Fagotto and Basson

Jérémie Papasergio is dedicated to the research of old reed instruments from the Renaissance to the Classical period.

Philippe Jaroussky

Countertenor

Philippe Jaroussky performs entirely in falsetto register. He has said that his natural singing voice is in the baritone range.
